# Product Mission

## Pitch
Lecture Auto is a terminal-based lecture-note automation application that helps college students capture and organize learning content without missing important points by providing seamless recording-to-markdown workflows with clear, usable summaries.

## Users

### Primary Customers
- University Students: Students who attend live or online lectures and want to reduce manual note-taking burden while improving review quality.
- Study-Focused Learners: Learners preparing for exams who need fast, structured summaries from long lecture recordings.

### User Personas
**Undergraduate Student** (19-25)
- **Role:** Full-time college student managing multiple classes
- **Context:** Balancing lectures, assignments, and part-time responsibilities
- **Pain Points:** Missed details during class, inconsistent notes, time-consuming review
- **Goals:** Capture every lecture, produce clean notes quickly, and study more effectively before exams

## The Problem

### Lecture Notes Are Incomplete And Time-Heavy
Students often split attention between listening and writing, which leads to incomplete notes and weak recall. This can increase review time significantly and reduce confidence before quizzes and exams.

**Our Solution:** Convert lecture audio into structured, readable notes with optional transcript cleanup and summary generation tailored to study needs.

## Differentiators

### End-to-End Study Workflow In One Tool
Unlike manual note-taking apps or standalone transcription tools, we provide a full local path from recording to refined transcript to summary notes in one CLI workflow. This results in faster review preparation and more consistent note quality across classes.

## Key Features

### Core Features
- **Lecture Recording Automation:** Capture lecture sessions reliably so students can focus on listening instead of multitasking.
- **Audio Transcription:** Turn recordings into searchable text that makes lecture content easier to revisit.
- **Summary Note Generation:** Produce concise study notes in either user-selected formats or automatic formats for quick review.

### Collaboration Features
- **Shareable Markdown Notes:** Let students share generated `.md` notes with classmates for group study and comparison.

### Advanced Features
- **Context-Aware Transcript Refinement:** Improve transcript readability with better spacing, wording, and corrections so notes are easier to understand.
- **Flexible STT/LLM Configuration:** Support local or API-based models so users can choose the setup that fits privacy, cost, and quality needs.
